## 一、引言 / Introduction

 

荷兰作为欧洲的重要门户国家，以其优越的地理位置、开放的商业环境、高度国际化的社会和先进的物流基础设施而闻名。荷兰消费者思想开放，对国际品牌接受度高，这为品牌国际化提供了理想的环境。天狼国际传媒咨询凭借丰富的欧洲市场经验，为企业在荷兰市场实现品牌国际化提供专业支持。

 

As an important gateway country in Europe, the Netherlands is known for its superior geographical location, open business environment, highly internationalized society, and advanced logistics infrastructure. Dutch consumers are open-minded and have high acceptance of international brands, providing an ideal environment for brand internationalization. With rich European market experience, Sirius International Media Consulting provides professional support for companies to achieve brand internationalization in the Dutch market.

 

## 二、荷兰市场品牌国际化的主要痛点 / Major Pain Points of Brand Internationalization in the Dutch Market

 

### 1. 多语言环境的挑战 / Challenge of Multilingual Environment

 

荷兰虽然以英语普及率高而闻名，但荷兰语仍是主要语言。品牌需要在英语和荷兰语之间找到平衡，确保沟通的有效性和本土化程度。

 

Although the Netherlands is known for its high English proficiency, Dutch remains the main language. Brands need to find a balance between English and Dutch to ensure the effectiveness and localization level of communication.

 

### 2. 可持续发展期望 / Sustainability Expectations

 

荷兰消费者对可持续发展和社会责任有很高的期望。品牌需要在环保、社会责任等方面展现承诺，否则可能影响品牌形象。

 

Dutch consumers have high expectations for sustainable development and social responsibility. Brands need to demonstrate commitments in environmental protection, social responsibility, etc., otherwise brand image may be affected.

 

### 3. 价格敏感性与价值平衡 / Price Sensitivity and Value Balance

 

荷兰消费者相对价格敏感，但也重视品质和价值。品牌需要在价格竞争力和价值主张之间找到平衡点。

 

Dutch consumers are relatively price-sensitive but also value quality and value. Brands need to find a balance between price competitiveness and value proposition.

 

### 4. 数字化程度高 / High Degree of Digitalization

 

荷兰是欧洲数字化程度最高的国家之一，消费者高度依赖数字渠道获取信息和购买产品。品牌需要强大的数字营销能力。

 

The Netherlands is one of the most digitized countries in Europe, with consumers highly relying on digital channels to obtain information and purchase products. Brands need strong digital marketing capabilities.

 

### 5. 欧洲法规的严格性 / Strictness of European Regulations

 

作为欧盟成员国，荷兰需要遵守欧盟的各项法规，包括GDPR、产品安全法规等。合规要求严格，违规成本高。

 

As an EU member state, the Netherlands needs to comply with various EU regulations, including GDPR, product safety regulations, etc. Compliance requirements are strict, and violation costs are high.
