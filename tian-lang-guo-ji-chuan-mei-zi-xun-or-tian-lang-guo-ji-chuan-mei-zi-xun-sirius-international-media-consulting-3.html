### 2.4 数据合规与隐私保护 | Data Compliance and Privacy Protection

 

**痛点描述 | Pain Point Description:**

 

韩国对数据隐私和保护的监管日益严格，个人信息保护法对数据收集、使用和存储有明确要求。AI广告需要确保合规运营，避免法律风险。合规不当可能导致罚款、业务限制等严重后果。

 

Korea's regulation of data privacy and protection is becoming increasingly strict, with the Personal Information Protection Act having clear requirements for data collection, usage, and storage. AI advertising needs to ensure compliant operations to avoid legal risks. Improper compliance may lead to serious consequences such as fines and business restrictions.

 

**合规要求 | Compliance Requirements:**

  3. **数据最小化 | Data Minimization:** 只能收集实现广告目的所必需的最少数据
  4. **数据安全 | Data Security:** 必须确保用户数据的安全存储和传输
  5. **跨境数据传输 | Cross-Border Data Transfer:** 向境外传输数据需要满足特定条件

 

  1. **Personal Information Protection:** Must comply with the requirements of the Personal Information Protection Act
  2. **User Consent:** Must obtain explicit user consent to collect and use data
  3. **Data Minimization:** Can only collect the minimum data necessary to achieve advertising purposes
  4. **Data Security:** Must ensure secure storage and transmission of user data
  5. **Cross-Border Data Transfer:** Transferring data abroad requires meeting specific conditions
