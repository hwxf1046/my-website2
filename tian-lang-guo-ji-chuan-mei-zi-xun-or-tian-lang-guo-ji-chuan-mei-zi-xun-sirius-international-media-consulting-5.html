## 二、核心决策痛点深度解析 | II. In-Depth Analysis of Core Decision Pain Points

 

### 2.1 投资模式选择决策 | Investment Mode Selection Decisions

 

**痛点描述 | Pain Point Description:**

 

企业在丹麦市场投资时，面临多种投资模式的选择：绿地投资、并购、合资、战略联盟等。每种模式都有其优缺点和适用条件，企业往往难以做出最优决策。投资模式选择不当可能导致资源浪费、整合困难、风险增加。

 

When investing in the Danish market, enterprises face choices among multiple investment modes: greenfield investment, acquisition, joint venture, strategic alliance, etc. Each mode has its advantages, disadvantages, and applicable conditions, and enterprises often struggle to make optimal decisions. Improper investment mode selection may lead to resource waste, integration difficulties, and increased risks.

 

**模式分析 | Mode Analysis:**

 

  1. **绿地投资 | Greenfield Investment:** 从零开始建立业务，完全控制但周期长
  2. **并购 | Acquisition:** 收购现有企业，快速获得市场份额但整合难度大
  3. **合资 | Joint Venture:** 与本地企业合作，分担风险但控制权分享
  4. **战略联盟 | Strategic Alliance:** 建立战略合作关系，灵活性高但控制力有限

 

  1. **Greenfield Investment:** Build business from scratch, complete control but long cycle
  2. **Acquisition:** Acquire existing enterprises, quickly gain market share but high integration difficulty
  3. **Joint Venture:** Cooperate with local enterprises, share risks but shared control
  4. **Strategic Alliance:** Establish strategic cooperation relationships, high flexibility but limited control
