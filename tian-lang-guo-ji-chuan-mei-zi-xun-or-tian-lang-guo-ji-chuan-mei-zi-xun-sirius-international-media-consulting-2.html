## 一、冰岛市场企业营销现状分析 | I. Analysis of Current Icelandic Market Enterprise Marketing Status

 

### 1.1 市场特征与机遇 | Market Characteristics and Opportunities

 

冰岛拥有37万人口，人均GDP超过7万美元，是北欧人均收入最高的国家之一。冰岛市场具有以下特征：经济稳定、消费能力强、环保意识极强、数字化程度高、市场相对封闭。冰岛的旅游和可再生能源产业为市场提供了独特的机遇。

 

Iceland has a population of 370,000 and a per capita GDP exceeding $70,000, making it one of the highest per capita income countries in the Nordic region. The Icelandic market has the following characteristics: stable economy, strong consumption power, extremely strong environmental awareness, high digitalization, and relatively closed market. Iceland's tourism and renewable energy industries provide unique opportunities for the market.

 

### 1.2 中国企业在冰岛市场的表现 | Performance of Chinese Enterprises in Icelandic Market

 

近年来，中国对冰岛的投资持续增长，涵盖旅游、能源、科技、渔业等多个领域。然而，中国企业在冰岛市场的营销效果并不理想，主要问题集中在品牌认知度低、文化理解不足、可持续发展理念缺失等方面。数据显示，仅有约18%的中国企业在冰岛市场建立了有效的品牌影响力。

 

In recent years, Chinese investment in Iceland has continued to grow, covering multiple fields including tourism, energy, technology, and fisheries. However, the marketing effectiveness of Chinese enterprises in the Icelandic market is not ideal, with main issues concentrating on low brand awareness, insufficient cultural understanding, and lack of sustainable development concepts. Data shows that only about 18% of Chinese enterprises have established effective brand influence in the Icelandic market.
