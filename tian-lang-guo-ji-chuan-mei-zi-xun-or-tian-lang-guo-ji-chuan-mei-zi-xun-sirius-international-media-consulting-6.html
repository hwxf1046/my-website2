## 二、核心决策痛点深度解析 | II. In-Depth Analysis of Core Decision Pain Points

 

### 2.1 市场进入模式选择 | Market Entry Mode Selection

 

**痛点描述 | Pain Point Description:**

 

企业在进入荷兰市场时，面临多种进入模式的选择：直接出口、设立代表处、建立子公司、并购、合资等。每种模式都有其优缺点和适用条件，企业往往难以做出最优决策。选择不当可能导致资源浪费、风险增加、错失市场机遇。

 

When entering the Dutch market, enterprises face choices among multiple entry modes: direct export, establishing representative offices, setting up subsidiaries, acquisition, joint venture, etc. Each mode has its advantages, disadvantages, and applicable conditions, and enterprises often struggle to make optimal decisions. Improper selection may lead to resource waste, increased risks, and missed market opportunities.

 

**模式分析 | Mode Analysis:**

 

  1. **直接出口 | Direct Export:** 成本低、风险小，但市场控制力弱
  2. **代表处 | Representative Office:** 便于市场探索，但业务范围有限
  3. **子公司 | Subsidiary:** 完全控制，但投资大、风险高
  4. **并购 | Acquisition:** 快速获得市场份额，但整合难度大
  5. **合资 | Joint Venture:** 分担风险，但控制权分享

 

  1. **Direct Export:** Low cost, low risk, but weak market control
  2. **Representative Office:** Facilitates market exploration but limited business scope
  3. **Subsidiary:** Complete control, but high investment and high risk
  4. **Acquisition:** Quickly gain market share, but high integration difficulty
  5. **Joint Venture:** Share risks, but shared control
