## 一、引言 / Introduction

 

丹麦作为北欧的创新中心，以其强大的创新能力、可持续发展理念、高质量的生活和开放的商业环境而闻名。丹麦虽然市场规模相对较小，但消费者购买力强，对创新和可持续产品接受度高，是许多国际企业投资北欧市场的首选。天狼国际传媒咨询凭借丰富的北欧市场经验，为企业提供专业的丹麦市场投资决策支持。

 

As the innovation center of Northern Europe, Denmark is known for its strong innovation capabilities, sustainable development concepts, high quality of life, and open business environment. Although the Danish market size is relatively small, consumers have strong purchasing power and high acceptance of innovative and sustainable products, making it the first choice for many international companies to invest in the Nordic market. With rich Nordic market experience, Sirius International Media Consulting provides professional Danish market investment decision support for enterprises.

 

## 二、丹麦市场投资的主要决策痛点 / Major Decision-Making Pain Points for Investment in the Danish Market

 

### 1. 市场规模与投资回报平衡 / Market Size and Investment Return Balance

 

丹麦人口约580万，市场规模相对有限。企业需要评估投资规模与市场潜力的匹配度，确保合理的投资回报率。

 

Denmark has a population of about 5.8 million, and the market size is relatively limited. Companies need to evaluate the match between investment scale and market potential to ensure a reasonable return on investment.

 

### 2. 创新与可持续发展的双重压力 / Dual Pressure of Innovation and Sustainable Development

 

丹麦市场对创新和可持续发展都有极高的期望。品牌需要在产品创新和可持续发展方面同时展现承诺，这对企业资源和能力提出了更高要求。

 

The Danish market has extremely high expectations for both innovation and sustainable development. Brands need to demonstrate commitments in both product innovation and sustainable development, which places higher requirements on company resources and capabilities.

 

### 3. 数字化转型与渠道变革 / Digital Transformation and Channel Transformation

 

丹麦是欧洲数字化程度最高的国家之一，传统零售渠道受到电商的强烈冲击。企业需要适应数字化转型，建立强大的线上渠道能力。

 

Denmark is one of the most digitized countries in Europe, with traditional retail channels being strongly impacted by e-commerce. Companies need to adapt to digital transformation and establish strong online channel capabilities.

 

### 4. 高运营成本与盈利压力 / High Operating Costs and Profitability Pressure

 

丹麦的运营成本相对较高，特别是人力成本和办公成本。企业需要优化成本结构，确保盈利能力。

 

Denmark's operating costs are relatively high, especially labor costs and office costs. Companies need to optimize cost structures to ensure profitability.

 

### 5. 法规合规的严格性 / Strictness of Regulatory Compliance

 

作为欧盟成员国，丹麦需要遵守欧盟的各项法规，同时也有本国的特定法规。合规要求严格，违规成本高。

 

As an EU member state, Denmark needs to comply with various EU regulations while also having its own specific regulations. Compliance requirements are strict, and violation costs are high.
